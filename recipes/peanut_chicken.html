Peanut Butter Chicken

Description:

A savory peanutty salty and tangy sauce on chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 lb skinless boneless chicken breast halves - cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 7 fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1- 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes with juice
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 3/4 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 3 tbs crushed garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • soy sauce to taste
  • red wine vinegar to taste
  • sesame oil to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces, and cook until chicken starts to turn white. Add the onion, mushrooms, and red pepper flakes. Season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring constantly until onoins are translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Pour the tomatoes and chicken stock into the skillet, and simmer for about 10 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through. Stir in peanut butter, and cook stirring constantly until the sauce thickens. This should only take a minute or two. If the sauce is not thickening, you may stir in more peanut butter. If this is not the case, you may not.
  3. If you wish more tang, salt, and nuttiness, add soy sauce, red wine vinegar, sesame oil to taste. Be careful to do so only one ingredient at a time, and only by small amounts at once.
